Who Can Apply for Study at Reach

They are from:

  • Poor family
  • Victims of violence, gender inequality or discrimination (Living with disability; Violence victim, trafficked victim, single mother, LGBT+, Ethnic minority)
  • Youth lack of family care
  • At risk youth
  • Economically affected youth

    • What do they study at REACH

    IT

    Video Editing

    Teaching students on how to use sotfware to design and edit videos

    3D Modeling

    Creating 3D models using 3D Smax sotfware.

    Web coding

    Facilitating a basic coding platform using PHP.

    Graphic Design

    Learning curriculum: software introduction, basic web designing. Applicable to jobs in graphic companies

    HOSPITALITY

    Hospitality - 5 Star

    REACH collaborates with 5 stars hotels in Ha Noi and Ho Chi Minh city to support students with practical training of different fields within the hotels.

    Food and Beverage

    Learning curriculum: Cleaning, serving plates, plating, receiving orders and preparing.
    Applicable to jobs such as hotel staff, waiter/waitress and bartender

    Housekeeping

    Learning curriculum: Room cleaning, customer serving, hygiene and safety assuring.
    In charge of room services in hotels and guest houses.

    Cooking

    Supporting students who love cooking and working inside kitchens.

    OTHERS

    Hairdressing & Nail

    Supporting students of basic hair styling and nail painting skills

    Spa Theapy

    Helping youths to be successful in the ever-expanding beauty industry

    Marketing & Sales

    Learning curriculum: Skills and ethics needed in the industry, CRM (customer relationship management), the variety of products, inventory management, inventory control, currency change

    The Interests of Students
    • Are supported from 70%-100% tuition fees.
    • Are equipped with free short courses in English, Computer Science, Life Skills ...
    • Focus on practicing more than learning the theory.
    • Other supporting including uniforms, computers.
    • Especially, 100% of students will be introduced to jobs after completing the course.
    • Beside the professional major knowledge, students will be improved their soft skills such as teamwork, positive thinking,  green skills, job interviewing skills, personal financial management, reproductive health care, etc.), extracurricular activities
    Enrollment Process
    STEP 1
    STUDENT RECORD
    To enroll in REACH, students need to prepare a full record sent via the post office or submit it directly.
    Records include
    Curriculum vitae as certified by the local authority
    Copy of birth certificate
    Photo ID
    Application for vocational training. Copy of their highest educational certificate.
    2 portraits size 2x3
     Priority papers (if they have them)
    STEP 2
    INTERVIEW
    After that, Students will go to REACH to have an interview.
    STEP 3
    HOMEVISIT
    Next, REACH will review the interview records, visit the student's houses, verify their information and give the final results.
